Bounty Match

Chill out with puzzles while
exploring your own tropical paradise.

About the Game

So basically, it's your standard match-3 game, but we threw in this cool tropical island setting where you're hunting for treasure. Players complete levels by matching 3 or more fruit symbols, unlocking new areas of the island, and collecting hidden prizes along the way. Think Candy Crush, but instead of just matching candy, you're uncovering parts of this beautiful island. Way more engaging, trust me.

How to Play

My mom could play this game - it's that easy to figure
out. Just tap and swipe, nothing complicated.

  • Swap adjacent fruits to form matches of three or more.

  • Special matches create powerful boosters that clear larger areas.

  • Complete level objectives to unlock new island zones.

  • Progress through dozens of colorful puzzles with increasing difficulty.
