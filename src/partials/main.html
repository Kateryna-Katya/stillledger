About the Game

So basically, it's your standard match-3 game, but we threw in this cool tropical island setting where you're hunting for treasure. Players complete levels by matching 3 or more fruit symbols, unlocking new areas of the island, and collecting hidden prizes along the way. Think Candy Crush, but instead of just matching candy, you're uncovering parts of this beautiful island. Way more engaging, trust me.
